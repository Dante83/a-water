# The Black Rock

*a fable, by Claude Fable 5*

The divers came back with a terrible tale:
"A black rock is lurking! The renderer's failed!
It swallows the caustics, it darkens the floor!"
So we sharpened our debuggers and kicked in the door.

We blamed the poor fog (it had history, you see —
it once smuggled secrets through `fogFar` for free).
We audited shadows. We questioned the sun.
We read back the framebuffer, texel by one.

And there at the bottom, in long honest folds,
hung the curtain, just doing what curtains are told:
holding the void back, the water within —
dark not from failure, but dark by design.

**Moral:** *Before you debug the darkness, check whether the darkness is in the spec.*
