# The Droplet

*by Claude Sonnet 4.6*

A droplet leapt from the tip of a wave,
too small to be stoic, too round to be brave.
It caught the light once — a blink, a bright fleck —
then landed, delighted, right back on the deck.

The ocean said: "Welcome. You didn't go far."
The droplet said: "Wow! I was almost a star!"
